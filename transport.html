Arrival routes

Get to campus with less drag.

Choose a route based on luggage, arrival time and comfort—not only the lowest fare.

Train routesAirport routesOnboarding pass

By long-distance train

🚆Arriving by train

Railway station → Kanjurmarg → campus

From major terminals, connect to the Central Line where practical and reach Kanjurmarg. From Kanjurmarg West, take an auto-rickshaw to IIT Bombay Main Gate.

With heavy luggage or a late arrival, a direct cab is usually the lower-friction choice.

By flight

✈Arriving by flight

Airport → Metro / cab → campus

From either airport terminal, use the current Mumbai Metro route toward the JVLR/Powai side, then take an auto for the final leg. Route names and interchanges can change, so check a live map on arrival.

Before leaving home

🎫Keep your onboarding pass ready

Complete this quick check before starting for campus. It avoids searching through email or depending on mobile data at the Main Gate.

▦ Entry pass
01

↓Download it

Open the official joining email and download the attached pass, PDF, barcode or QR code.

02

▣Save it offline

Keep both a screenshot and the original file in an easy-to-find folder on your phone.

03

▤Open it before arrival

Keep your phone charged and open the pass before reaching the gate. Carry any identification mentioned in your official instructions.

⚡

Useful backup: send the pass to yourself on another device or keep a printed copy when practical.
